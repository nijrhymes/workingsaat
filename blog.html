We are SAAT- By WizTab Uzoma

By | January 02, 2019 | 3 comments

Let's Show Them That We Are From SAAT, FUTO. Don't think we don't work... It is absolutely unbelievable that our nation Nigeria, will totally abandon things pertaining to Agriculture. So many years ago when petroleum or oil has not been discovered, Agriculture was at the peak in our source of income as a nation. People are living happily without feeling the pressures of hunger and hardship. The the discovery of petroleum turned this great nation into a nation full of leaders without innovative ideas, fraudulent activities and money conscious citizens. Leaders no longer invest on Agriculture. Even the word "Agriculture" has gone outdated in the ears of an average Nigerian man. This negligence has caused more harm than good. Some Youths left thing concerning Agriculture for old people like fathers and mothers. They think that such activity is not meant for youths. This idea is totally wrong. Without Agriculture, no food would be put to your dinning table. People will always die of hunger. In the absence of Agriculture, humans will not exist. Even the biblical indication of Adam and Eve gave a recognition to Agriculture (Garden of Eden). God has his reasons of placing Agriculture for the survival of men.
